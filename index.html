WE ARE DATA RESPONSIBLY

Nowhere is the need to control the power and to judiciously use technology more apparent than in massive data analysis, known as big data. Our goal is to understand how legal and ethical norms of society can be embedded in technology, and to build tools that make data sharing, collection and analysis responsible.
